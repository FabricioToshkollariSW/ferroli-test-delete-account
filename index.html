How to delete
your account

Note: This action is irreversible. Once your account has been deleted, all associated data will be lost and removed from our database.

Delete procedure

  • Login

    Enter the Email address, enter the Password and press the LOGIN button

  • Profile

    In the menu at the bottom, select the PROFILE section

  • Delete

    Press the DELETE PROFILE button

  • Confirm

    Confirm the deletion by pressing DELETE
